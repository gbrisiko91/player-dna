# PlayerDNA Premium Model

## 1. Free Tier (Base DNA)
- Access to the 30-question quiz.
- Basic Result Card (Shareable).
- 1 Saved analysis history.
- Global Leaderboard access.

## 2. Pro Tier ($9.99 / Lifetime)
- **Advanced Neuro-Report**: 15 pages of psychological analysis on in-game behavior.
- **Mental Coaching AI**: Suggestions based on your DNA to reduce tilt and improve clutch factor.
- **Comparative Analysis**: Compare your DNA with pro players or streamers.
- **Animated Card Skins**: Exclusive high-fidelity animated card designs for social sharing.
- **Discord Private Channel**: Access to the 'Alpha Players' community.

## 3. Team Tier ($49 / Month)
- **Synergy Mapping**: Analyze how a team's DNAs interact (e.g., "Do you have too many Main Characters?").
- **Conflict Prediction**: Predict which players are likely to clash during high-stress matches.
- **Recruitment Tool**: Find players with specific archetypes to balance your roster.
